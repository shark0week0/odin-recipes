Mapo Tofu

Description

I was inspired after my trip to China to make mapo tofu. Still a work in progress.

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu, cut and pressed of excess water
  • 1/2 tbsp sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
  • 1 lb minced pork
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp dobajan (Chinese chili bean paste)
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tsp shaoxing wine
  • 1 tsp light shoyu
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch and 1 tbsp water for slurry
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Fry minced pork for 2 minutes on medium high heat.
  2. Fry dobajan so oil is infused red, 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add stock, shoyu, shaoxing wine, sugar, and simmer 3 minutes.
  4. Add tofu, bring up heat. Cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add vinegar, mix. Add slurry, mix.
  6. Add sesame oil.
  7. Top with peppercorns, green onions. Serve hot.
