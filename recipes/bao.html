Char Siu Bao

Description

I still haven't really figured out how to keep the buns closed after steaming.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast (1 packet)
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the filling:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • Bottle of char siu sauce

Steps

Filling:

  1. Mix sauce and pork until combined.

Dough:

  1. Combine water, sugar, yeast. Let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Add 1 cup of flour at a time, mixing with rest of ingredients.
  3. Knead until smooth. Cover, let rise 1 hour.
  4. Punch down, let sit 5 minutes.
  5. Divide into 10 balls. Roll each ball into 5-inch circle.
  6. Place spoon of filling in middle of circle. Bring up sides of dough, pinching and sealing with a twist.
  7. Repeat for rest of balls.

Steam:

  1. Arrange buns on parchment paper in bamboo steamer.
  2. Add water to skillet (make sure water will not reach buns once steamer is placed inside) and bring to boil.
  3. Steam 15 minutes, then shut off heat and let cool 10 minutes. DO NOT OPEN THE STEAMER.
  4. Once it's had time to finish cooking, open steamer and serve still warm.
